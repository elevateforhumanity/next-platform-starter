## Dependent Care Narrative

### a) Dependent Care Payment Rates

Dependent care reimbursements are provided in accordance with the Child Care and Development Block Grant (CCDBG) guidance and based on local market rate surveys conducted in Marion County, Indiana. Reimbursement rates reflect current market conditions:

- **Infant care (0-2 years):** $180-220 per week
- **Preschool care (3-5 years):** $140-180 per week
- **School-age care (6+ years):** $100-150 per week

Participants may receive direct payment assistance to approved licensed childcare providers or reimbursement for documented dependent care expenses incurred while participating in SNAP E&T activities. Payments are made based on actual usage and documented service delivery.

### b) Dependent Care Waiting List Management

If dependent care providers have waiting lists or otherwise cap the number of enrolled dependents, Elevate for Humanity will ensure SNAP E&T participants with dependent care needs receive childcare through the following mechanisms:

1. **Alternative Provider Network:** Elevate maintains a list of approved alternative dependent care providers with available capacity to accommodate participant needs.

2. **Flexible Scheduling:** Elevate coordinates with participants and training providers to offer flexible program scheduling that accommodates available childcare options in the community.

3. **Subsidy Application Assistance:** Elevate assists participants in applying for childcare subsidies through partner agencies and state-level dependent care assistance programs.

4. **Backup Childcare Arrangements:** In cases where community providers have limited capacity, Elevate will work with contracted backup childcare providers to ensure continuity of care.

5. **Participant Support Coordination:** Elevate's case managers work directly with participants experiencing childcare barriers to identify solutions and maintain program engagement.

**Outcome:** No SNAP E&T participant will be exempted from the program due to unavailable childcare. Elevate commits to securing appropriate dependent care arrangements for all participants with dependent care needs.
