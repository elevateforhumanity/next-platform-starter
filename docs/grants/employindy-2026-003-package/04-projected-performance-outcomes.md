## Credential Attainment Strategy

EPA 608 Universal Certification is the anchor credential for this program. It is:

- Federally recognized by the EPA under Section 608 of the Clean Air Act
- Industry-required — no HVAC technician may legally handle refrigerants without it
- Stackable toward BOMA, IFMA, and Indiana postsecondary occupational programs
- Administered by an approved third-party testing organization
- Covered under WIOA supportive services (no cost to participant)

Elevate's LMS checkpoint system requires participants to pass readiness assessments before exam registration, ensuring only exam-ready participants sit — maximizing pass rates and credential attainment percentages.
