# RFP 2026-003 — WIOA In-School Youth Service Provision

# WIOA 14 Service Elements Delivery Plan

---

## Element 1 — Tutoring, Study Skills & Dropout Prevention

**Delivery Method:** Elevate's LMS provides academic support modules covering study skills, time management, and workplace readiness. Career Coach Ameco Martin identifies participants at risk of dropout through weekly LMS activity monitoring and coordinates with Warren Central Intervention Specialist Nicole Simpson for school-based academic intervention and referral to IPS academic recovery resources.

**Staff Responsible:** Career Coach, Case Manager, Nicole Simpson (Warren Central)

---

## Element 2 — Alternative Secondary School Services / Dropout Recovery

**Delivery Method:** For participants who have dropped out or are at risk, Career Coach coordinates with Warren Central administration and IPS alternative education resources to support re-enrollment or alternative diploma pathways. Elevate does not deliver alternative secondary services directly but maintains active referral relationships with IPS alternative education programs.

**Staff Responsible:** Career Coach, Warren Central administration

---

## Element 3 — Paid & Unpaid Work-Based Learning _(PRIMARY INVESTMENT AREA)_

**Delivery Method:** This is Elevate's primary investment area and core competitive strength.

- **Pre-Apprenticeship:** HVAC Building Services Technician pre-apprenticeship through Elevate's DOL-registered RAP (Provider ID 206251). Participants complete online RTI via Elevate's LMS and OJT hours at 2Exclusive Platinum Cleaning.
- **Job Shadowing:** Structured job shadowing placements at 2Exclusive Platinum Cleaning and Marion County HVAC employer partners, coordinated by Career Coach.
- **Summer Employment:** Summer OJT placements at 2Exclusive for participants completing the pre-apprenticeship pathway.
- **Employer Coordination:** Career Coach manages employer onboarding, placement documentation, OJT hour tracking, and mentor assignments.
- **Transportation Support:** Transportation stipends provided for all work-based learning participation.
- **WBL Tracking:** All OJT hours tracked via signed timesheets entered in Indiana Career Connect and Elevate's LMS dashboard.

**Staff Responsible:** Career Coach, Case Manager, 2Exclusive Platinum Cleaning mentors

---

## Element 4 — Occupational Skills Training

**Delivery Method:** HVAC Building Services Technician curriculum delivered via Elevate's LMS — 432 seat hours covering refrigeration systems, EPA 608 exam preparation, electrical fundamentals, HVAC controls, and preventive maintenance. EPA 608 Universal Certification is the primary industry-recognized credential. All curriculum is aligned with Indiana's Demand Occupation List.

**Credential:** EPA 608 Universal Certification (federally recognized, industry-required)
**Staff Responsible:** Career Coach, LMS platform (AI-assisted delivery)

---

## Element 5 — Education Concurrent with Workforce Preparation

**Delivery Method:** Elevate's LMS delivers HVAC technical content concurrently with employability skills, career exploration, and financial literacy — integrated into a single participant journey rather than sequential silos. Participants build occupational knowledge and workplace readiness simultaneously, reducing time-to-employment.

**Staff Responsible:** Career Coach, LMS platform

---

## Element 6 — Leadership Development

**Delivery Method:**

- **Employability Bootcamp:** Workplace behavior, professionalism, communication, and conflict resolution delivered via LMS and in-person sessions at Warren Central.
- **Financial Literacy:** Module covering budgeting, banking, credit, and tax basics — informed by Elizabeth Greene's background as an IRS Enrolled Agent and tax-services operator.
- **Employer-Led Sessions:** 2Exclusive Platinum Cleaning leadership delivers workplace expectations and professional development sessions for participants.
- **Team-Based Projects:** LMS includes collaborative learning activities building accountability and peer leadership skills.

**Staff Responsible:** Career Coach, Elizabeth Greene (financial literacy), 2Exclusive leadership

---

## Element 7 — Supportive Services

**Delivery Method:** All supportive services are covered at no cost to participants under WIOA funding:

- Transportation stipends for work-based learning participation
- Work attire and tools for OJT placements
- EPA 608 exam fees covered
- Multilingual support and accessibility accommodations
- Participant milestone incentives
- Referrals to community resources for housing, food, childcare, and mental health as needed

**Staff Responsible:** Case Manager, Career Coach

---

## Element 8 — Adult Mentoring (minimum 12 months)

**Delivery Method:** Each participant on the HVAC pathway is assigned a qualified journeyworker mentor at 2Exclusive Platinum Cleaning. Mentors provide daily on-site supervision during OJT, weekly one-on-one check-ins, and competency milestone sign-offs. Career Coach Ameco Martin serves as the primary mentoring relationship for participants not yet in OJT placements. All mentoring relationships are maintained for a minimum of 12 months from program entry.

**Staff Responsible:** Career Coach (primary), 2Exclusive journeyworker mentors (OJT phase)

---

## Element 9 — Follow-Up Services (minimum 12 months post-exit)

**Delivery Method:** Case manager conducts structured follow-up contact at 30, 90, 180, and 365 days post-exit. Follow-up covers employment status, earnings, education enrollment, and barrier identification. All follow-up data entered in Indiana Career Connect to support WIOA 2Q and 4Q retention measures. Participants retain the right to opt out of follow-up services per WIOA requirements.

**Staff Responsible:** Case Manager

---

## Element 10 — Comprehensive Guidance & Counseling

**Delivery Method:** Career Coach provides individualized career and academic counseling through the ISS process. For participants with mental health, substance abuse, or trauma-related needs, Career Coach coordinates referrals through Warren Central Intervention Specialist Nicole Simpson and community behavioral health partners. Elevate does not provide clinical counseling directly but maintains active referral pathways.

**Staff Responsible:** Career Coach, Nicole Simpson (Warren Central), community referral partners

---

## Element 11 — Financial Literacy

**Delivery Method:** Financial literacy module delivered via Elevate's LMS covering budgeting, banking, credit building, tax basics, and financial goal-setting. Content is informed by Elizabeth Greene's background as an IRS Enrolled Agent and operator of IRS-aligned tax preparation services. Participants learn to read a pay stub, understand withholding, and build a basic budget before entering employment.

**Staff Responsible:** Career Coach, LMS platform, Elizabeth Greene (content development)

---

## Element 12 — Entrepreneurial Skills Training

**Delivery Method:** Entrepreneurship module covering small business basics, self-employment pathways, gig economy navigation, and business registration fundamentals. Delivered via Elevate's LMS with supplemental in-person sessions. Connects to Elizabeth Greene's experience as a multi-business founder and IRS Enrolled Agent.

**Staff Responsible:** Career Coach, LMS platform, Elizabeth Greene (content development)

---

## Element 13 — Labor Market Information

**Delivery Method:**

- Sector-based HVAC career exploration cohorts at Warren Central
- Industry tours at 2Exclusive Platinum Cleaning and Marion County HVAC employer sites
- LMI sessions covering Indiana Demand Occupation List, HVAC wage data, and career ladder from pre-apprentice to journeyworker to facilities manager
- Career Coach delivers labor market context in every ISS development session

**Staff Responsible:** Career Coach, 2Exclusive Platinum Cleaning

---

## Element 14 — Postsecondary Preparation & Transition

**Delivery Method:**

- FAFSA completion support for participants pursuing postsecondary education
- Dual credit guidance in coordination with Warren Central counseling staff
- Postsecondary bridge coaching for participants transitioning to Ivy Tech, IUPUI, or other Indiana institutions
- EPA 608 certification positioned as a stackable credential toward BOMA, IFMA, and Indiana postsecondary occupational programs
- Career transition plans developed in ISS for every participant

**Staff Responsible:** Career Coach, Case Manager, Warren Central counseling staff
