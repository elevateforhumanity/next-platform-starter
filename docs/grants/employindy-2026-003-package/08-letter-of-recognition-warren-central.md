Dear Principal Taylor,

On behalf of Elevate for Humanity, I am pleased to formally recognize Warren Central High School and MSD Warren Township as a valued partner in our proposal for EmployIndy RFP 2026-003 — WIOA In-School Youth Service Provision.

Elevate for Humanity is a Marion County nonprofit workforce development organization and existing EmployIndy and WorkOne partner. We hold active DOL Registered Apprenticeship Program status (Provider ID 206251) and operate a proprietary AI-enabled Learning Management System delivering career training and credentialing pathways to Indianapolis residents.

Through this partnership, Elevate for Humanity commits to delivering the following services to Warren Central High School students at no cost to the school or its students:

- A dedicated Career Coach (Ameco Martin) embedded at Warren Central during school hours
- WIOA eligibility screening and enrollment for students ages 16–19 with documented barriers
- Individual Service Strategy (ISS) development for every enrolled participant
- HVAC pre-apprenticeship pathway leading to EPA 608 Universal Certification — a federally recognized, industry-required credential
- All 14 WIOA Youth Service Elements including career exploration, financial literacy, leadership development, work-based learning, and postsecondary transition support
- Transportation stipends, work attire, and exam fees covered at no cost to participants
- 12-month follow-up services for all program completers

We recognize Warren Central High School as a school that serves a diverse student population including youth with documented barriers to employment and postsecondary success. This partnership is designed to connect your students directly to high-demand career pathways, living-wage employment, and industry-recognized credentials in Marion County's growing HVAC and building services sector.

We are grateful for the opportunity to partner with Warren Central High School and look forward to working with you, Intervention Specialist Nicole Simpson, and your staff to support the workforce success of your students.
