## Overview

Elevate Course Factory generates complete, accreditation-aligned curriculum that can be licensed to schools, workforce providers, apprenticeship sponsors, and training organizations.

This document defines the licensing tiers, intellectual property framework, and go-to-market strategy for selling curriculum as a product.

---

## Why Curriculum Licensing

**The Market Need**:
- Most small trade schools, workforce providers, and community organizations lack curriculum developers
- Apprenticeship sponsors need complete curriculum with RTI/OJL tracking
- States require documented curriculum for licensing approval
- Employers want standardized training aligned to certifications

**Elevate's Advantage**:
- Complete blueprints with modules, lessons, assessments, competencies
- Built-in apprenticeship tracking (RTI, OJL, employer evaluations)
- Certification alignment (EPA 608, CCMA, CNA, Barber, etc.)
- Interactive LMS integration
- White label capability

## License Types

### 1. Single School License

**Best for**: One-location schools, community colleges, training centers

**Terms**:
- One physical or virtual location
- Up to 100 active students
- One instructor seat included
- Curriculum updates for 12 months
- Email support

**Pricing**:
- Setup: $2,500
- Monthly: $99/month
- Annual: $990/year (17% savings)

**Includes**:
- Full curriculum download (PDF + editable)
- LMS access for 100 students
- Instructor resource package
- Student workbooks (digital)

**What's NOT included**:
- Additional locations
- Additional instructor seats ($49/seat/month)
- Customization
- White labeling

### 2. Multi-Site License

**Best for**: School districts, workforce boards, statewide programs

**Terms**:
- Up to 5 locations
- Up to 500 active students
- Up to 10 instructor seats
- Curriculum updates for 24 months
- Phone + email support
- Quarterly check-in calls

**Pricing**:
- Setup: $5,000
- Monthly: $299/month
- Annual: $2,990/year (17% savings)

**Includes**:
- All Single School License features

### 4. Apprenticeship License

**Best for**: Registered apprenticeship sponsors, DOL programs, employer consortiums

**Terms**:

- OJL (On-the-Job Learning) tracking
- Employer evaluation forms
- Competency sign-off workflow
- RAPIDS reporting integration
- DOL compliance documentation

**Pricing**:
- Setup: $5,000
- Monthly: $399/month (includes up to 50 apprentices)
- Additional apprentices: $5/apprentice/month

**Includes**:
- All curriculum with apprenticeship layer
- Employer portal access
- Apprentice tracking dashboard
- RTI/OJL hour logging
- Competency verification workflow
- Employer evaluation templates
- RAPIDS-ready reporting

- Full customization rights
- Reseller rights available

**Pricing**:
- Setup: $15,000
- Monthly: $1,499/month
- Annual: $14,990/year

**Includes**:
- All Enterprise License features
- Full white label implementation
- Custom colors and branding
- Your domain exclusively
- Reseller agreement option

## Go-to-Market Strategy

### Target Customers

**Tier 1 — Immediate**:
- Workforce development boards
- Registered apprenticeship sponsors
- Community colleges
- Nonprofits serving reentry populations

**Tier 2 — Short Term**:
- Trade schools
- Hospital systems (for CNA, MA training)
- Salon/spa owners (for apprenticeship programs)
- Union training programs

**Tier 3 — Long Term**:
- School districts (CTE programs)
- State workforce agencies
- Franchise operations
- Enterprise employers

### Why They Buy

| Customer | Pain Point | Solution |
|---------|-----------|----------|
| Workforce Board | Need curriculum for grant programs | Ready-to-use, compliant |
| Apprenticeship Sponsor | DOL requires documented RTI | Complete RTI curriculum + tracking |
| Small Trade School | Can't afford curriculum developers | Affordable licensing model |
| Community Nonprofit | Need credentials for clients | Certification-aligned curriculum |
| Employer | Need standardized training | Competency-based, employer portal |

### Sales Motion

1. **Demo School Environment** — Show working LMS with curriculum
2. **Needs Assessment** — Understand their program requirements
3. **Curriculum Mapping** — Show how our content maps to their needs
4. **License Recommendation** — Suggest appropriate tier
5. **Pilot Program** — 30-day pilot with 10 students
6. **Full Rollout** — Full license activation
