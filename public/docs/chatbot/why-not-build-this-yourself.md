# Why Not Build This Yourself?

## The Hidden Costs of DIY Learning Management

### Executive Summary

Building an in-house learning management system seems straightforward until you account for the true costs: development time, compliance requirements, ongoing maintenance, and opportunity cost. This document outlines why partnering with Elevate for Humanity delivers better outcomes at lower total cost.

---

## The Real Cost Breakdown

### Development Costs

| Component                          | DIY Estimate            | Timeline         |
| ---------------------------------- | ----------------------- | ---------------- |
| Core LMS functionality             | $150,000 - $300,000     | 6-12 months      |
| Compliance modules (WIOA, FERPA)   | $75,000 - $150,000      | 3-6 months       |
| Reporting & analytics              | $50,000 - $100,000      | 2-4 months       |
| Mobile responsiveness              | $25,000 - $50,000       | 1-2 months       |
| Integrations (Calendly, CRM, etc.) | $30,000 - $60,000       | 2-3 months       |
| **Total**                          | **$330,000 - $660,000** | **14-27 months** |

### Ongoing Maintenance

- Annual maintenance: 20-25% of initial build cost
- Security updates and patches: Continuous
- Compliance updates (regulations change): Quarterly
- Staff to maintain: 1-2 FTEs minimum

## The Opportunity Cost

Every month spent building is a month not serving learners.

- **6 months of development** = 6 months of delayed impact
- **Staff diverted to IT** = Staff not focused on mission
- **Budget on technology** = Budget not on programs

---

## Decision Framework

### Build if:

- You have unique requirements no platform can meet
- You have dedicated IT staff with LMS experience
- Timeline is not a constraint
- Budget exceeds $500,000 for technology alone

### Partner if:

- You need to launch within 30-90 days
- Compliance is a priority
- You want to focus resources on learners, not technology
- You need proven outcomes, not experiments
