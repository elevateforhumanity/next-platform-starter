# Welding Technology

## Program Syllabus

**Provider:** Elevate for Humanity  
**ETPL Provider ID:** 10000949  
**Program Code:** WELD-TECH  
**Duration:** 12-24 Weeks (480-960 Hours)  
**Delivery Method:** Hands-On Lab Training  
**Funding:** Workforce Grants • Apprenticeship • WIOA

---

## Program Overview

Master a high-demand skilled trade! Learn MIG, TIG, stick welding, blueprint reading, and metal fabrication through intensive hands-on training. Program prepares for AWS certification and immediate employment in manufacturing, construction, automotive, and industrial settings. Welders earn $40K-$60K+ starting.

---

## Learning Objectives

1. Perform SMAW (stick), GMAW (MIG), and GTAW (TIG) welding
2. Read and interpret welding blueprints and symbols
3. Select appropriate welding processes and materials
4. Apply welding safety and PPE protocols
5. Inspect welds for quality and defects
6. Use cutting and fabrication equipment
7. Pass AWS certification tests
8. Troubleshoot welding equipment and processes

---

## Course Outline

### Module 1: Welding Safety & Fundamentals (40 hours)

- OSHA 10-Hour Construction Safety
- Welding safety and PPE
- Arc welding hazards
- Fire prevention and hot work permits
- Ventilation and fume control
- Electrical safety
- Shop equipment and tools

### Module 2: Oxyfuel Cutting & Plasma (60 hours)

- Oxyfuel equipment setup
- Cutting techniques
- Plasma arc cutting
- Safety procedures
- Metal preparation
- Cutting quality and troubleshooting

### Module 3: SMAW - Shielded Metal Arc Welding (Stick) (120 hours)

- SMAW equipment and setup
- Electrode selection and handling
- Flat position welding
- Horizontal position welding
- Vertical position welding
- Overhead position welding
- Fillet and groove welds
- Weld quality and inspection

### Module 4: GMAW - Gas Metal Arc Welding (MIG) (120 hours)

- GMAW equipment and setup
- Wire and gas selection
- Short circuit transfer
- Spray transfer
- Flat and horizontal welding
- Vertical and overhead welding
- Aluminum welding basics
- Troubleshooting and adjustments

### Module 5: GTAW - Gas Tungsten Arc Welding (TIG) (120 hours)

- GTAW equipment and setup
- Tungsten electrode preparation
- Filler metal selection
- Steel welding techniques
- Stainless steel welding
- Aluminum welding
- All position welding
- Precision and quality control

### Module 6: Blueprint Reading & Welding Symbols (40 hours)

- Reading technical drawings
- Welding symbols and specifications
- Dimensions and tolerances
- Material specifications
- Weld joint design
- Fabrication drawings

### Module 7: Metal Fabrication (80 hours)

- Layout and measurement
- Cutting and shearing
- Bending and forming
- Grinding and finishing
- Assembly and fit-up
- Fabrication projects

### Module 8: Weld Inspection & Quality Control (40 hours)

- Visual inspection techniques
- Weld defects and causes
- Destructive testing
- Non-destructive testing (NDT) overview
- Quality standards (AWS, ASME)
- Documentation and certification

### Module 9: Advanced Welding & Certification Prep (80-320 hours)

- Pipe welding
- Structural welding
- Specialized processes
- AWS certification test preparation
- Practice tests and evaluations
- Career readiness
