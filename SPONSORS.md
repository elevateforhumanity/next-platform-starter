# Support Elevate for Humanity

Elevate for Humanity provides free workforce training to underserved communities in Indiana.
Programs are funded through WIOA, Workforce Ready Grant, and DOL-registered apprenticeships.
Your support directly covers training costs, credentialing fees, and learner support services.
