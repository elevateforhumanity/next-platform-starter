# Partnership Outreach Wave Plan

## Sending Rules

- Max 10-12 recipients per wave (BCC)
- Max 2-3 waves per day
- Send Tuesday through Thursday, 9:00-10:30 AM ET
- Use BCC for all recipients — never CC
- Send from your primary Elevate email, not a bulk sender
- Subject line: "Partnership Inquiry — Elevate for Humanity (Workforce Training)"

## Follow-Up Plan

- **Day 3 after each wave:** If no reply, send a one-line follow-up:
  "Hi — following up on my note about a workforce training partnership. Happy to answer any questions or set up a brief call. — Elizabeth"

- **Day 7:** If still no reply, mark as "no response" and move on. Do not send a third email.

- **Responses:** Any reply (positive or negative) gets a personal response within 24 hours. Do not auto-reply.

---

## Tracking

After sending, log responses in the contact-list.csv by adding a "Response" column:

- "Interested" — schedule call
- "Not now" — note and revisit in 90 days
- "No response" — after follow-up, archive
- "Unsubscribe" — remove permanently
