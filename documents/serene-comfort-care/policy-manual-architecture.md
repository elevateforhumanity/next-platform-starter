## 3.8 Internal Review Procedures

**Purpose:** Establish a formal internal checkpoint that ensures all intake documents are reviewed for completeness and compliance before external submission or processing.
**Policy:** Serene Comfort Home Care LLC does not automatically submit client records, intake forms, or authorization requests to any outside agency. All documents pass through an internal administrative review step. Documents found to be incomplete or non-compliant are returned for correction before external processing.
**Procedure:**
1. Intake staff collects all required documents
2. Supervisor or designated reviewer performs completeness check using internal checklist
3. Any gaps are flagged and resolved before forwarding
4. Reviewer logs the review date and initials on the internal checklist
5. Only after internal approval does the document proceed to external payer/agency
**Responsible Staff:** Intake Coordinator, Administrator
**Required Documentation:** Internal review checklist, reviewer log with dates and initials

> **TRAINING PARTNERSHIP NOTICE — READ BEFORE POPULATING THIS SECTION:**
> Serene Comfort Home Care LLC has a formal workforce training partnership with Elevate for Humanity. Elevate for Humanity delivers pre-employment training, apprenticeship curriculum, and continuing education modules via its LMS platform. However:
> - Elevate for Humanity training does NOT substitute for competency verification by Serene Comfort Home Care LLC clinical staff
