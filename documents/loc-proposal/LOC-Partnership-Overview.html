About Elevate for Humanity

Elevate for Humanity is an Indianapolis-based Career & Technical Institute and DOL Registered Apprenticeship Sponsor. We deliver industry-aligned credential pathways in healthcare, skilled trades, CDL, and technology — each designed for working adults and funded workforce participants. We are listed on Indiana's Eligible Training Provider List (ETPL) and approved under WIOA Title I and the Workforce Ready Grant.

Alignment with LOC's 2026 Workforce Outcomes

Based on your confirmed targets, here is how Elevate's model maps to LOC's deliverables:

27

Participants Enrolled in Trades-Related Career Training

We can accommodate a single cohort of 27 or structure as two cohorts (15 + 12) across HVAC and a second program. Enrollment can begin within 4–6 weeks of signed agreement.

24

Participants Completing with a Credential

Our completion model targets 85%+ credential attainment. Bi-weekly check-ins, absence alerts, and bilingual tutoring support are built in to reduce attrition. Credentials are issued by national certifying bodies — portable and verifiable.

20

Participants Employed at $14+/hr Within 18 Months

Our employer pipeline in Indianapolis targets $18–$28/hr starting wages for HVAC, Electrical, and Plumbing graduates. We track 90-day and 180-day placement with employer wage verification and report results to LOC monthly.

Proposed Launch Timeline — HVAC First Cohort

1

Week 1–2 — Agreement & Intake

MOU / partnership agreement signed. LOC refers participants. Elevate conducts intake screening with LOC coaching team. Cohort confirmed (15–27 participants).

2

Week 3–4 — Enrollment & Orientation

Participants enrolled in LMS. Orientation completed (online, ~2 hours). Equipment and materials distributed. LOC coaching team granted dashboard access.

3

Weeks 5–12 — Classroom Phase (RTI)

Mon/Wed evenings, 5:30–8:30 PM. 6–8 weeks of classroom instruction. Bi-weekly progress reports to LOC. Absence alerts within 24 hours. EPA 608 exam administered on-site.

4

Weeks 13–20 — OJT Phase

Participants placed at employer partner sites in Indianapolis. 160+ supervised hours. LOC coaching team receives weekly status updates. OJT hours documented for apprenticeship registration.

5

Week 20+ — Credential Issuance & Placement

Credentials issued. Job placement support activated. 90-day and 180-day follow-up tracking begins. Monthly employment reports delivered to LOC through 18-month outcome window.
