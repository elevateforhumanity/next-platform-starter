# Elevate for Humanity Career and Training Institute

## Workforce Referral Partner Sheet

---

### Program Purpose

Elevate for Humanity is a workforce training institute designed to prepare adults for immediate placement into in-demand occupations through short-term, outcome-driven training.

---

### Programs Offered

Career training aligned with high-demand occupations and employer hiring needs.

---

### Program Length

Short-term, workforce-aligned training with defined start and end dates.

---

### Credential Earned

Industry-recognized credential and/or certificate of completion aligned with occupational standards.

---

### Tuition

**$5,000 per participant**

Tuition may be fully or partially covered through workforce funding programs.

---

### Funding Alignment

Programs are structured to align with workforce funding eligibility, including referrals from American Job Center partners and local workforce boards.

---

### Ideal Participants

- Unemployed or underemployed adults
- Career switchers
- Dislocated workers
- Justice-involved individuals
- Adults seeking rapid re-entry into the workforce

---

### Employer Alignment

Training is designed in coordination with employer demand to support rapid placement and retention.

---

### Capacity

Cohort-based enrollment with limited seats to ensure quality outcomes.

---

### Reporting & Compliance

We provide referring agencies with:

- Enrollment confirmation
- Attendance verification
- Completion status
- Placement updates

---

### Referral Process

1. Case manager submits referral
2. Student completes intake and eligibility screening
3. Enrollment confirmation issued
4. Ongoing communication through completion
